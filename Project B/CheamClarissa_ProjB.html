Please use a browser that supports "canvas"


For the axis: red = X, green = Y, blue = Z.

Open the JavaScript Console, please: (right-click-->'Inspect Element'-->'Console' tab).

To rotate the winged heart, click and drag on the canvas.
To aim left/right, press left/right arrow.
To aim up/down, press the up/down arrows.
To strafe left, press D.
To strafe right, press A.
To move towards the aim point, press W.
To move backwards from the aim point, press S.

Mouse Drag totals (CVV x,y coords): 0,0

Current Quaternion: w + xi + yj + zk where (i,j,k) are 3-way imaginary numbers:
X=0i, Y=0j, Z=0k, W=1.
length=1.
